Something evil stirs in Arkham, and only you can stop it. Blurring the traditional lines between role-playing and card game experiences, Arkham Horror: The Card Game is a Living Card Game of Lovecraftian mystery, monsters, and madness!

In the game, you and your friend (or up to three friends with two Core Sets) become characters within the quiet New England town of Arkham. You have your talents, sure, but you also have your flaws. Perhaps you've dabbled a little too much in the writings of the Necronomicon, and its words continue to haunt you. Perhaps you feel compelled to cover up any signs of otherworldly evils, hampering your own investigations in order to protect the quiet confidence of the greater population. Perhaps you'll be scarred by your encounters with a ghoulish cult.

No matter what compels you, no matter what haunts you, you'll find both your strengths and weaknesses reflected in your custom deck of cards, and these cards will be your resources as you work with your friends to unravel the world's most terrifying mysteries.

Each of your adventures in Arkham Horror LCG carries you deeper into mystery. You'll find cultists and foul rituals. You'll find haunted houses and strange creatures. And you may find signs of the Ancient Ones straining against the barriers to our world...

The basic mode of play in Arkham LCG is not the adventure, but the campaign. You might be scarred by your adventures, your sanity may be strained, and you may alter Arkham's landscape, burning buildings to the ground. All your choices and actions have consequences that reach far beyond the immediate resolution of the scenario at hand—and your actions may earn you valuable experience with which you can better prepare yourself for the adventures that still lie before you.

Release Types

  1. Core Set: Required for everything. Includes 239 cards, 149 tokens, 2 rule books and 1 campaign guide with 3 scenarios.
    • A second core set is required to play with 3-4 players, as well as 2 players in certain combinations of investigators with basic card decks.
  2. Deluxe Expansion: Includes additional investigators, player cards, encounter cards, location cards and 2 scenarios which begin a cycle.
  3. Mythos Pack: Includes additional player cards, encounter cards, location cards and 1 scenario which continues a cycle.
    • A copy of the cycle's deluxe expansion is required to play the scenario.
  4. Upgrade Expansion: Includes alternate/supplementary scenario cards and new encounter cards for an already-released campaign cycle, along with new player cards and storage accessories (box and dividers).
  5. Investigator Starter Deck: Includes a fully pre-built and ready-to-play 34-card investigator deck, as well as 26 additional cards that can be used to upgrade your deck as you play through a campaign.
  6. Scenario Pack: Includes encounter cards and location cards for 1 stand-alone scenario, with optional instructions for integrating it into a campaign.
    • Sometimes also referred to as "print-on-demand (PoD) scenarios" due to being manufactured in-house by FFG.
  7. Print and Play Scenarios: Include printable special challenge scenarios, each designed for a specific investigator as a side story in a campaign or as a standalone scenario. Each scenario also includes the alternative version of that existing investigator, known as parallel investigators.
  8. Promo Cards: Player cards released as part of a promotion, available when attending Organised Play or when purchasing another item for example.
    • Includes previews of new investigators, investigator cards with alternative artwork and replacement signature cards for existing investigators.
