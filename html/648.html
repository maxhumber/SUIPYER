Players are Arena-Fight managers who compete for the job of Overall Arena-Fight manager in Rome. They can get this job by supporting the right candidate for becoming Emperor.

Organizing Gladiator fights and entertaining the crowd will win the players money and the candidate they support Popularity Points. Whether a fight was a commercial success or not depends on the supporters of the Gladiator teams, because they get to vote after each fight (thumbs up or down) and losing supporters/gladiators will be ejected from the game.

Colorful counters, good subject, eventful and interactive game.

Community Wiki

Edit

Vox Populi allows you to be the president of one of the greatest Roman Gladiator clubs. Your job is to organize matches in which your gladiators will bring glory and honor upon your club. By doing so better than the others you will be appointed by the Emperor as sole organizer of the Roman Circus Games. Bribery is the most efficient way; give your best gladiators to the future Emperor - if you know who he is?

The components are simply superb. The counters come in a variety of shapes and sizes, the map is mounted, resin pyramids and beautiful cards round out the components. The rules are only 12 pages, making it possible to begin play shortly after opening the box.

Components:

1 Mounted game board
55 Cards
1 Popularity chart
6 Resin cast coliseum miniatures
1 Rule book
6 Gladiator club miniatures
2 countersheets
1 six sided die
3-6 players ages 8 and up
Playing Time: 90 minutes
